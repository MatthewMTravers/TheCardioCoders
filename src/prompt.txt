Workout Plan​
Goal: Muscle Gain​
Duration: 8 weeks​
Training Split: Upper/Lower​
​
​
Day 1: Chest and Triceps​
Exercise 1: Barbell Bench Press​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 8 reps​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Keep your core tight, maintain proper form, avoid rushing the motion.​
* Instructions: Slowly lower the weight, control the motion, and engage the target muscle.​
Exercise 2: Incline Dumbbell Press​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps​
* Rest Time: 30-60 seconds​
* Key Tips: Focus on squeezing your chest muscles at the top of the movement.​
* Instructions: Keep your back straight, push through your heels.​
Exercise 3: Tricep Pushdown​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Extend your arm fully, then lower the weight.​
* Instructions: Keep your elbow close to your body, avoid swinging.​
​
​
Day 2: Back and Biceps​
Exercise 1: Pull-ups​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of as many reps as possible​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Engage your core, pull yourself up with your lats.​
* Instructions: Keep your body straight, avoid swinging.​
Exercise 2: Barbell Rows​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 8 reps​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Focus on squeezing your back muscles at the top of the movement.​
* Instructions: Keep your core tight, pull the weight towards your chest.​
Exercise 3: Dumbbell Bicep Curls​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps​
* Rest Time: 30-60 seconds​
* Key Tips: Squeeze your biceps at the top of the movement.​
* Instructions: Keep your upper arms still, focus on curling the weight.​
​
​
Day 3: Legs​
Exercise 1: Squats​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 8 reps​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Focus on explosive movement during the concentric phase.​
* Instructions: Keep your back straight, push through your heels.​
Exercise 2: Leg Press​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps​
* Rest Time: 30-60 seconds​
* Key Tips: Engage your core, extend your leg fully.​
* Instructions: Keep your feet flat on the platform, avoid leaning forward.​
Exercise 3: Lunges​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps (per leg)​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Focus on balance and control during the movement.​
* Instructions: Keep your front knee at a 90-degree angle, avoid letting your back knee extend past your toes.​
​
​
Day 4: Shoulders and Abs​
Exercise 1: Shoulder Press​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 8 reps​
* Rest Time: 60-90 seconds​
* Key Tips: Focus on squeezing your shoulder muscles at the top of the movement.​
* Instructions: Keep your core tight, avoid swinging.​
Exercise 2: Lateral Raises​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 10 reps​
* Rest Time: 30-60 seconds​
* Key Tips: Engage your core, keep your elbows close to your body.​
* Instructions: Keep your arms straight, avoid swinging.​
Exercise 3: Plank​
* Sets & Reps: Hold for 30-60 seconds​
* Rest Time: N/A​
* Key Tips: Focus on engaging your core, maintaining a straight line from head to heels.​
* Instructions: Keep your body stable, avoid wobbling.​
​
​
Day 5: Rest​
​
​
Day 6: Cardio and Abs​
Exercise 1: Treadmill or Stationary Bike​
* Sets & Reps: 30 minutes of steady-state cardio​
* Rest Time: N/A​
* Key Tips: Focus on maintaining a consistent pace, avoid fatigue.​
* Instructions: Keep your posture upright, engage your core.​
Exercise 2: Russian Twists​
* Sets & Reps: 3 sets of 12 reps​
* Rest Time: 30-60 seconds​
* Key Tips: Engage your core, twist your torso fully.​
* Instructions: Keep your feet flat on the floor, avoid swinging.​
​
Day 7: Rest​
​
​Day 8: Review and Rest​
